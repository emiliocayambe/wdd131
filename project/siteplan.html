Site Plan: Horizonte 593

1. Site Name

Horizonte 593

I chose this name because "Horizonte" evokes the vast views captured by a drone, and "593" is Ecuador's country code. This combination gives the site a distinct local identity while sounding professional, modern, and tech-oriented.

  • Optional domain availability: horizonte593.com

    • 2. Site Purpose

    The purpose of "Horizonte 593" is to serve as a digital flight log and visual portfolio for aerial photography. It provides a platform to organize, display, and manage drone footage and flight data (locations, dates, and flight types). The site also allows users to register new flight activities and contact the pilot for potential videography services or location suggestions.

    3. Scenarios

    • "I am a tourist looking for beautiful places to visit in Ecuador. Where can I see high-quality aerial photos of beaches or mountains to decide my next trip?"
    • "I am a drone enthusiast. What specific gear (drone model, weight, range) was used to capture these shots, and what safety checks should I perform before flying?"
